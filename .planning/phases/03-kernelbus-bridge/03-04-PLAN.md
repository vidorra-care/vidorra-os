<objective>
Wire `KernelBusHost` into the Shell. Two integration points:
1. `WindowFrame.tsx` — add `iframeRef`, call `registerFrame` on mount and `unregisterFrame` on unmount (D-02, D-03)
2. `main.tsx` — call `kernelBusHost.init(callbacks)` once at startup, providing `useWindowStore` action callbacks (dependency injection pattern from Plan 02)

Purpose: Without this wiring, no iframe can become trusted and no RPC calls can work. Satisfies the "Shell-side trust registration" part of BUS-01 and enables BUS-02 end-to-end.
Output: Shell initializes KernelBusHost at startup; each WindowFrame registers/unregisters its iframe with the host.
</objective>
